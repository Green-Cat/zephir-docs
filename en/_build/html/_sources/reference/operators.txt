Operators
=========
Zephir's set of operators are similar to the ones PHP and also inherits some of their behaviors.

Arithmetic Operators
--------------------
The following operators are supported:

+-------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| Operation         | Example                                             |
+-------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| Negation          | -a                                                  |
+-------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| Adition           | a + b                                               |
+-------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| Substraction      | a - b                                               |
+-------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| Multiplication    | a * b                                               |
+-------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| Division          | a / b                                               |
+-------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
